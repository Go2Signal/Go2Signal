PRIVACY POLICY FOR Go2Signal

Last updated: May 03, 2021

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Go2Signal (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by Leitmannstetter Engineering. The Application is searching your address book for all useres that use the messanger Signal and/or messanger WhatsApp. Based on this results it shows you how many of your user have Singal and/or WhatsApp installed. You can define a for you required percentage of your contacts that must have Signal installed before you move completely to Signal. Also you can define users that must have installed Signal (so called "Key Users") before you move completely to Signal. Is the required percentage reached and all Key Users have installed Signal this App will inform you that it is time to make Signal your only messenger.

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application reads your address book and stores in the Application all users with WhatsApp and/or Signal. This information is used to display in the Application how many of your contacts have installed WhatsApp and/or Singal. Also this information is used to inform you if the, in the Application Settings defined, percentage is reached and all Key Users, also in the Application Settings defined, have installed Signal. All information read in the address book is only used in the Application and will not be uploaded to any external server.

What are my opt-out rights?

You can stop all collection of information by the Application easily by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of your mobile device or via the mobile application marketplace or network.
